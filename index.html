The City

A Look At Where and When We Got Mugged, Murdered and Maimed In The City By the Bay

Summer 2014

To go along with the unseasonably warm temperates that year, there were 32,754 instances of reported crime in The City. With over 10,000 incidents per month and 300 per day, the SFPD had their hands full.

The data set is publicly available and comes from DataSF. While some may have qualms about the state of law and law-enforcemnt in The City, there is little doubt that the SFPD public data is among the finest and best maintained in the world.

Thanks too to those who have contributed and have helped make this a more open, connected world. Finally, thanks to those who have empowered people to interpret, dissect, and display the chaos around them.
